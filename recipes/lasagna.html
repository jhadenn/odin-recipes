Lasagna

Description

This recipe creates the cheesiest tastiest lasagna ever #nocap

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 300 degrees
  2. Grab a pan and lay noodles meat sauce and cheese
  3. Place in oven for 30 minutes
  4. Enjoy the lasagna
